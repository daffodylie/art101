Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem Solving

Challenge

For this lab, we were asked to pseudocode an everyday task with our partner. Then, we pseudocoded Rock-Paper-Scissors, and turned that pseudocode into comments in javascript. Lastly, we edited this HTML file to reflect our efforts.

Problems

This was a pretty simple assignment, and pretty much everything worked without issue. The only problem I experienced was the inability to left-justify the javascript comments on this page. It isn't that big of a deal for it to be left-justied, so I'm fine to leave it as is.

Reflection

I felt good about this assignment, and am looking forward to learning more about javascript in the coming weeks. It was fun to come up with the steps for Rock-Paper-Scissors with my partner Valarie.

Results

Here is our outline for brushing your teeth, incase you didn't know how to do it:

  1. Find a place with water
  2. Grab your toothbrush
  3. Run toothbrush under water
  4. Squeeze toothpaste onto toothbrush
  5. Brush your teeth
  6. Rinse out your mouth
